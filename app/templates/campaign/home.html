Fiscal Stability

We are OFF the deficit list!

First balanced budget in 10 years.

We still need a plan for long-range fiscal stability.



Student Achievement


NEW: Balanced calendar at Holmes Elementary shortens the summer break, spreading learning more evenly throughout the year.

NEW: Washtenaw International Middle Academy (WIMA), an International Baccalaureate school, prepares students for the rigors of high school and college.

NEW: Partnership with Washtenaw Community College offers career certificates in Health and Construction trades, and the ability to earn college credit.


We still need:

More Pre-K and Young 5 programs.
More career credential programs.
More balanced calendar schools.
An IB school at the elementary level.



We Are Working to Change the Culture


Small learning communities function as a “school within a school” and help create safe and supportive learning environments.

Restorative practices teach students to take responsibility for their actions.

We are committed to building deep and meaningful relationships with students and families by actively learning about their lives and honoring all cultures.

We still need Family Resource Centers to support families and students.
